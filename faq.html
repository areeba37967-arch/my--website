Frequently Asked Questions

❓ How do I place an order?

You can place an order by visiting our bakery, calling us, or using the Contact page on our website.

🎂 Can I customize my cake?

Yes! We create fully customized cakes based on your theme, color, size, and design preferences.

🚚 Do you offer delivery?

Yes, we provide same-day delivery in Islamabad & Rawalpindi for selected orders.

💳 What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, bank transfer, JazzCash, EasyPaisa and major debit/credit cards.

📅 How early should I order?

Birthday cakes: 1 day before.
Wedding cakes: At least 7 days before.

🥚 Do you make eggless cakes?

Yes! Eggless and gluten-free cakes are available on advance request.
