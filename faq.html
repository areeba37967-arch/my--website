Do you deliver?

Yes! We deliver all over Islamabad with free delivery on orders above 5000 PKR.

For custom cakes, please order at least 3 days in advance. Regular cakes can be ordered 24 hours before.

Absolutely! We specialize in custom designs. Just share your idea and we will make it happen.
