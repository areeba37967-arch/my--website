FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I order?

+

We recommend ordering at least 3-5 days in advance for custom cakes. For wedding or large event cakes, please order 2-3 weeks ahead to ensure availability and perfect execution.

What flavors do you offer?

+

We offer chocolate, vanilla, strawberry, red velvet, lemon, carrot cake, and many more. Custom flavor combinations are also available upon request!

Do you deliver?

+

Yes! We deliver across major cities in Pakistan. Delivery charges may vary based on your location. Contact us for specific delivery details.

Can I customize my cake design?

+

Absolutely! We specialize in custom designs. Share your ideas, photos, or themes, and our team will create a unique cake just for you.

What are your payment options?

+

We accept cash on delivery, bank transfers, and online payments through JazzCash, Easypaisa, and credit/debit cards.
